How it works

Online therapy is as effective as in-office counseling. It saves your relationships, money and time.
Tell us about your concerns by filling in a short questionnaire.
We’ll match you with the best counselors to address your problem.
Schedule an online talk at a time convenient to you.

Therapy without leaving home

Don’t waste time on commuting, internet connection is all you really need. Contact your therapist with a laptop, tablet, or smartphone.

Choose communication methods which are most convenient to you. Talk to your therapist through a phone or video session.

Online therapy from the comfort of your home will help you feel more at ease when discussing your personal issues and revealing your feelings.
